Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I create a chat room?

Simply click on "Create Room" in the navigation, enter your name, and you'll get a unique room code to share with friends.

Is HiThere free to use?

Yes! HiThere is completely free to use. You can create rooms, chat with friends, and enjoy all features without any cost.

How secure are my conversations?

We take privacy seriously. All conversations are encrypted and we don't store your messages permanently on our servers.

Can I use HiThere on mobile devices?

Absolutely! HiThere is fully responsive and works beautifully on all devices - phones, tablets, and desktops.
